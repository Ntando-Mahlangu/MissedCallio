  <p>At MissedCall.io, we want you to be completely satisfied with our service. This Refund Policy explains when and how refunds are issued.</p>

  <div class="highlight-box">
    <p><strong>Summary:</strong> We offer a 7-day free trial — no credit card required. If you experience a verified technical failure on our end within your first 30 days of paid subscription, we will issue a full refund for that month.   <h2>1. Free Trial</h2>
  <p>All new accounts receive a 7-day free trial with no credit card required. You will not be charged anything during your trial period. If you choose not to continue after your trial, simply do not add payment details — your account will be deactivated and you owe nothing.</p>

  <h2>2. 30-Day Satisfaction Guarantee</h2>
  <p>If you are not satisfied with MissedCall.io within your first 30 days of a paid subscription, contact us at <a href="mailto:hello@missedcallio.online">hello@missedcallio.online</a> and we will review your case. Refunds within the first 30 days are issued at our discretion and are typically approved when:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>You experienced a verified technical failure on our end that prevented the service from working.</li>
    <li>You were charged incorrectly due to a billing error.</li>
    <li>The service was materially different from what was described at the time of purchase.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>3. No Refunds After 30 Days</h2>
  <p>After 30 days of a paid subscription, we do not issue refunds for:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Partial months — if you cancel mid-month, your service continues until the end of the billing period and no partial refund is issued.</li>
    <li>Unused call allowance — if you did not use all your calls in a given month.</li>
    <li>Change of mind after 30 days.</li>
    <li>Failure to cancel before the next billing cycle.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>4. Technical Failures</h2>
  <p>If MissedCall.io experiences a significant technical outage that prevents the service from answering calls for more than 24 consecutive hours, you may be eligible for a pro-rated credit applied to your next billing cycle. We will notify affected customers proactively in the event of any major outage.</p>

  <h2>5. How to Request a Refund</h2>
  <p>To request a refund, email us at <a href="mailto:hello@missedcallio.online">hello@missedcallio.online</a> with:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Your full name and business name</li>
    <li>The email address associated with your account</li>
    <li>The reason for your refund request</li>
    <li>Any relevant details about the issue you experienced</li>
  </ul>
  <p>We aim to respond to all refund requests within 2 business days. Approved refunds are processed within 5–10 business days and returned to your original payment method via Paddle.</p>

  <h2>6. Cancellation</h2>
  <p>You may cancel your MissedCall.io subscription at any time by contacting us at <a href="mailto:hello@missedcallio.online">hello@missedcallio.online</a>. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. You will not be charged again after cancellation, but no refund will be issued for the remaining days in your current billing period.</p>

  <h2>7. Disputes</h2>
  <p>If you believe you have been charged incorrectly, please contact us before initiating a chargeback with your bank or card provider. We are committed to resolving billing issues quickly and fairly. Initiating a chargeback without first contacting us may result in immediate suspension of your account.</p>
